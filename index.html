About Me

I'm an experienced front-end developer with nearly 3 years of expertise in crafting stunning and responsive user interfaces. My proficiency in React and Next.js empowers me to create high-performance web applications that are both user-friendly and visually captivating.

Beyond React, I'm well-versed in Vue.js, allowing me to handle projects across multiple frameworks. I also have a strong command of web page development using CSS, SCSS, Bootstrap, and TailwindCSS, ensuring professional and consistent web page designs.

My passion for animations is evident through my use of Framer Motion and the Greensock Animation Platform (GSAP) to breathe life into designs. Additionally, I have hands-on experience with content management systems (CMS) like Sanity and Contentful, enhancing my ability to efficiently manage and deliver dynamic content.

I'm actively seeking a full-time role as a front-end developer and would welcome the opportunity to collaborate with those who value the creation of beautiful and responsive user interfaces.

Outside of coding, I indulge in writing fiction, exploring African literature, and delving into the realms of finance and economics.
