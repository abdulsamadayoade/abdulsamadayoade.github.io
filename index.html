About Me

I'm a skilled front-end developer with close to 3 years of experience building beautiful and responsive user interfaces. With a strong background in React, and Next.js, I build high-performance web applications that are both easy to use and visually appealing.

In addition to React, I also have experience with Vue.js, allowing me to tackle projects using multiple frameworks. I also posses a deep understanding of how to build web pages using CSS, SCSS, Bootstrap and TailwindCSS to make web pages look pixel perfect and consistent with the design.

I'm a lover of animations and I use Framer Motion/Greensock Animation Platform (GSAP) to bring designs to life.

I'm currently looking for a full-time role as a front-end developer. If you're looking for a developer who is passionate about building beautiful and responsive user interfaces, I'd love to hear from you!

While I'm not coding, you'll find me writing fiction, reading African literatures, or studying finance and economics.
